HandleScript Beta 2

In summary: HandleScript is effectively a group of modules made to make scripting in Scratch easier.

What does Beta 2 Add?

Beta 2 adds three features

  • Mode 2 for Stamper

    • Mode 2 is the same as mode 2 except that the end of the a list is read as a newline which makes it easier to modify data.

  • Mode 3 for Stamper

    • Mode 3 (aka: Tiles allows you to have two compress the two letter system to just one, all you have to do is define the alias and the actual symbol behind it.

  • Letter Excluder

    • Letter excluder allows you to remove letters from words or phrases.

How to make a VHS effect... Tutorial #1

What you'll need:

Paint.net

For the tutorial, I'm gonna be using this image

  • Step 1:

    Open your image in paint.net

  • Step 2:

    Duplicate your image layer (CTRL+SHIFT+D).

  • Step 3:

    Apply the Black & White effect (CTRL+SHIFT+G) to the lower layer and set its Brightness to 50 (CTRL+SHIFT+T).

  • Step 4:

    Set the Hue/Saturation (CTRL+SHIFT+U) effect to 200 on the upper layer and set its Brightness to 100 (CTRL+SHIFT+T).

  • Step 5:

    Squish the upper layer horizontally until its with is equal to itself divided by six (CTRL+A).

  • Step 6:

    Unsquish the upper layer until it horizontally fits the canvas again while using Bilinear (CTRL+A).

  • Step 7:

    Set the upper layer's blend mode to Multiply.

  • Step 8:

    Merge the two layers together and save it! (CTRL+SHIFT+G).
