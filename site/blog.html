How to make a VHS effect... Tutorial #1

What you'll need:

Paint.net

For the tutorial, I'm gonna be using this image

  • Step 1: Open your image in paint.net

  • Step 2: Duplicate your image layer CTRL+SHIFT+D

  • Step 3: Apply the Black & White effect CTRL+SHIFT+G to the lower layer and set its Brightness to 50 CTRL+SHIFT+T

  • Step 4: Set the Hue/Saturation CTRL+SHIFT+U effect to 200 on the upper layer and set its Brightness to 100 CTRL+SHIFT+T

  • Step 5: Squish the upper layer horizontally until its with is equal to itself divided by six CTRL+A

  • Step 6: Unsquish the upper layer until it horizontally fits the canvas again while using Bilinear CTRL+A

  • Step 7: Set the upper layer's blend mode to Multiply

  • Step 8: Merge the two layers together and save it! CTRL+SHIFT+G

Final
